One design note

Sage green stays the single action color — the way out is never louder than the calm. Destructive escape steps (Replace, Lost your token, broken-lock glyph) carry a muted warm clay #C8A27B so they read as “the exit,” not the path. No red except the one-line warning on irreversible code reveal.
